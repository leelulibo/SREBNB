About Us

At Spirit Revelation Church, we offer a tranquil retreat for those seeking spiritual renewal. Our accommodations are designed to provide comfort and peace, enhancing your experience whether you're here for a service, a retreat, or a peaceful escape. Join us to rejuvenate both body and spirit in a welcoming and serene environment. We look forward to making your stay memorable.
